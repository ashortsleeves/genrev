What is Gen Rev?

Gen Rev is a group, open to anyone, which organizes and leads trips, hikes, and events in the Maine Outdoors. It's free to participate! Check our Facebook Group or Calendar for trip & event info, and Email Us if you need a ride - we're all about carpooling.

"Gen Rev" stands for General Revelation, which is a theological term. It refers to the way God reveals Himself to us through nature and all of the things the human mind might discover just by living life on earth. (Special Revelation, on the other hand, refers to God's direct Word to us, as in the Bible).

The Gen Rev Outdoor Club organizes hikes and other outdoor events to encourage getting outside, enjoying nature and each other, and being attentive to God speaking to us in little ways when we keep an eye out for Him. Gen Rev has ties to New Hope Christian Reformed and Imago Dei Anglican Churches in Bangor, ME.
