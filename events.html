Annual BBQ Potluck & Games

Every summer Gen Rev gathers for a huge BBQ potluck with lots of food and games. We fire up three or four grills and share meat and sides. We play volleyball, whiffle ball, croquet, frisbee, relay races, pie eating contest and other fun games.

City Forest Walk

The Bangor City Forest is a hidden gem, 680 acres of wonderful wilderness right near the Bangor Mall area. Gen Rev gathers in the late spring each year for a Sunday afternoon walk.

Ski & Snowshoe Trips

Cold snowy weather is no reason to stay inside! Gen Rev loves taking trips to Maine ski mountains, or going snowshoeing or cross country skiing locally.
